A little about A*

A* is a graph traversal and pathfinding algorithm

The algorithm works by measuring 3 values:

  • f is the sum of g and h
  • g is the movement "cost" from the starting point to a given square on the grid
  • h is the estimated movement cost to move from that given square (f) to the final destination.

h is derived with a heuristic method. This algorithm is using the euclidean distance to get the absolute path from the start to the end. It then compares the cost of moving to the absolute distance from that cell. It always attempts to get a cost as low as possible, knowing that the lowest possible cost is the absolute path.
